Elon Musk uses Grok, the AI chatbot developed by his company xAI, as his primary assistant. He has also used ChatGPT in the past but is critical of OpenAI’s direction, leading to the creation of his own alternative.

# The Short Answer: Grok Is His Go-To

Elon Musk’s personal and professional chatbot of choice is Grok, the AI model developed by his company xAI. Launched in November 2023 after Musk’s growing dissatisfaction with OpenAI’s ChatGPT, Grok is designed to be more conversational, humorous, and uncensored. It’s available to X Premium+ subscribers and is integrated into the X (formerly Twitter) platform. Musk has repeatedly mentioned using Grok for various tasks, from answering queries to generating content. But the story doesn’t end there.

# Why Did Musk Create Grok?

Musk co-founded OpenAI in 2015 but left after disagreements about the organization’s direction and profit motives. He later criticized ChatGPT for being “woke” and overly cautious. In response, he formed xAI and built Grok with a focus on real-time knowledge, humor, and less restrictive guardrails. Musk himself uses Grok to demonstrate its capabilities and to test its limits—often in his own tweets. This hands-on approach has made Grok his default chatbot.

# Does Musk Ever Use Other Chatbots?

While Grok is his primary tool, Musk has acknowledged using ChatGPT in early 2023 and even praised its capabilities before his criticism mounted. He has also dabbled with other models like Google’s Gemini and Anthropic’s Claude for comparison, but these are not his daily drivers. Musk’s usage of ChatGPT was largely before Grok’s release; now, he publicly advocates for his own creation. In fact, he has repeatedly emphasized that xAI’s model is “the best” and that he “uses Grok every day.”

# The Competitive Landscape

Musk’s relationship with chatbots reflects his broader strategy in AI. He has warned about the dangers of AI but also accelerated his own development efforts. By using Grok, he sends a strong signal to the market. For a deeper look at how Grok stacks up against ChatGPT and other models, explore our detailed comparison of AI chatbots .

# Grok’s Unique Features That Appeal to Musk

  • Real-Time Knowledge: Grok has access to X’s data stream, giving it up-to-the-minute information—perfect for Musk’s fast-paced environment.
  • Unfiltered Tone: Grok is designed to be less politically correct, aligning with Musk’s free-speech advocacy.
  • Integration with X: As the owner of X, Musk benefits from tight integration, making Grok easily accessible for his daily interactions on the platform.

# Is Grok Available to Everyone?

Currently, Grok is exclusively available to X Premium+ subscribers ($16/month). Musk has hinted at broader availability in the future, but for now, it’s a premium perk. This exclusivity also allows Musk to use the chatbot as a testing ground for new features.

# The Future: What’s Next for Musk and Chatbots?

Musk has ambitious plans for Grok, including making it multimodal and integrating it into Tesla vehicles. He also mentioned that xAI is building a massive supercomputer to train future versions. As a power user and creator, Musk’s chatbot choice is likely to evolve, but Grok remains his primary tool as of 2026.

# Frequently Asked Questions

# H3: Does Elon Musk use ChatGPT?

Yes, he has used ChatGPT in the past, but since launching Grok, he rarely uses it publicly. He has expressed concerns about ChatGPT’s safety and bias, preferring his own model.

# H3: Is Grok better than ChatGPT according to Musk?

Musk believes Grok is superior in terms of humor, real-time information, and less censorship. However, independent benchmarks show mixed results; GPT-4 often outperforms Grok in reasoning tasks. Musk’s preference is partly driven by his business interests and philosophy.

# H3: Can I chat with the chatbot Elon Musk uses?

Yes, by subscribing to X Premium+, you can access Grok and interact with the same AI that Musk uses. However, the version Musk uses may have some internal enhancements not yet public.

In summary, Elon Musk’s favorite chatbot is unequivocally Grok—a decision rooted in both personal belief and business strategy. While he has experimented with others, Grok is his daily companion in the AI-powered world.
