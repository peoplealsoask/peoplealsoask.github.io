Connect Google Account to Notion: A Step-by-Step Guide

Learn how to connect your Google account to Notion for seamless integration

This integration enables you to access Google Drive files and calendar events directly within Notion.

Notion is an incredibly powerful tool for organizing your digital life, from notes and tasks to databases and calendars. One of its most useful features is the ability to integrate with other services, including Google. Connecting your Google account to Notion allows you to access Google Drive files, calendar events, and more, all from within the Notion app.

To get started, navigate to the Notion settings page and click on the ‘Google Drive’ or ‘Google Calendar’ option, depending on which service you want to connect. You’ll be prompted to authenticate your account and authorize Notion to access your Google data.

Once you’ve connected your Google account, you can start using the integration to access your Google Drive files and calendar events directly within Notion. For example, you can add a Google Drive file to a Notion page, or schedule a Notion task to appear on your Google Calendar.

# Setting up the Integration

To set up the integration, follow these steps:

  • Navigate to the Notion settings page
  • Click on ‘Google Drive’ or ‘Google Calendar’
  • Authenticate your Google account
  • Authorize Notion to access your Google data

You can also use Notion’s Google Drive integration to access your Google Drive files directly within Notion. This allows you to view, edit, and manage your Google Drive files without having to leave the Notion app.

# Using the Integration

Once you’ve set up the integration, you can start using it to access your Google Drive files and calendar events. Here are a few examples of how you can use the integration:

  • Add a Google Drive file to a Notion page
  • Schedule a Notion task to appear on your Google Calendar
  • View and edit your Google Drive files directly within Notion

# FAQ

# What are the benefits of connecting my Google account to Notion?

Connecting your Google account to Notion allows you to access Google Drive files and calendar events directly within Notion, making it easier to manage your digital life.

# Can I use the integration with multiple Google accounts?

Yes, you can use the integration with multiple Google accounts. Simply navigate to the Notion settings page and click on the ‘Google Drive’ or ‘Google Calendar’ option for each account you want to connect.
