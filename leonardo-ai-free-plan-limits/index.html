Executive Summary: Leonardo AI is not totally free; it offers a generous free plan with daily credits but with usage limits and restrictions on commercial use and advanced features.

# The Honest Answer: Is Leonardo AI Free?

No, Leonardo AI is not totally free. It operates on a freemium model. The free tier is a fantastic way to explore the platform, generate images, and test its capabilities—but it comes with clear constraints. Think of it as a trial: you get daily credits, access to some models, and standard resolution. To unlock unlimited generations, commercial licenses, and high-res exports, you’ll need to subscribe.

This article dives deep into what the free plan actually includes, where the limits lie, and whether it’s enough for your needs in 2026. Whether you’re a hobbyist, designer, or business owner, understanding these nuances will save you time and frustration.

# What Do You Get for Free? A Detailed Breakdown

# Daily Credits

Each day, free users receive 25 credits. Every generation costs 1 to 5 credits depending on resolution, model, and upscaling. You can earn extra credits by completing tasks, but for heavy users, 25 credits vanish quickly.

# Models & Features

  • Access to select community models (not all premium ones).
  • Standard upscaling (2x-4x).
  • Image generation at 512x512 to 1024x1024.
  • No access to private models or advanced fine-tuning.

# Commercial Use?

Images generated on the free plan are for personal, non-commercial use only. If you plan to sell or use them in any commercial project, you’ll need a paid subscription.

# Watermark & Attribution

Free images include a faint watermark (can be removed with credits) and require attribution if shared publicly.

# Hidden Limits You Should Know

  • Queue Priority: Paid users get faster generation. Free users may experience delays during peak hours.
  • Resolution Cap: Maximum 1024x1024 unless you spend credits on upscaling.
  • Archiving: Old images may be automatically deleted after a period if you don’t upgrade.
  • API Access: Not included in the free tier.

# Free vs Paid: What’s Worth Upgrading?

If you’re serious about AI art or need images for business, consider the paid plan. For $24/month (Artisan) or $48/month (Maestro), you get:

  • 7,500 to 25,000 credits per month
  • Full commercial license
  • Priority generation
  • Private workspace & higher resolution
  • Access to all models and features

But if you’re just experimenting or occasionally creating social media graphics, the free plan is surprisingly capable.

# Comparison With Other Free AI Image Generators

When you compare the best free AI image generators , Leonardo AI stands out for its quality and variety. However, services like Craiyon or Playground AI offer unlimited free generations with trade-offs in quality or speed. For high-quality outputs, Leonardo’s free tier is among the best.

# Tips to Maximize Your Free Credits

  1. Use lower resolutions: Sticking to 512x512 uses 1 credit instead of 4.
  2. Avoid upscaling unless necessary.
  3. Redo prompts strategically: Use a starting seed to iterate without wasting credits.
  4. Leverage daily login rewards.

# Frequently Asked Questions

# Is Leonardo AI completely free?

No, but it has a generous free plan with daily credits. For unlimited usage and commercial rights, you need a paid subscription.

# How many free credits do you get per day?

Free users receive 25 credits daily. Credits can be spent on generation, upscaling, and other features.

# Can I use images from the free plan commercially?

No. Free-tier images are for personal, non-commercial use only. A paid plan is required for commercial licenses.

# What happens if I run out of credits?

You’ll have to wait until the next day for a reset, or purchase additional credits or a subscription.

# Does the free plan have access to all models?

No. Free users get a curated selection of community models. Premium models are reserved for paid subscribers.
