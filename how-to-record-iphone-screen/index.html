To record your iPhone screen, you’ll need to add the Screen Recording feature to your Control Center. This can be done by going to Settings > Control Center, then tapping the ‘+’ icon next to Screen Recording. Once you’ve added the feature, you can open the Control Center and tap the Screen Recording icon, which looks like a white circle within another circle. You’ll then have the option to turn your microphone on or off. If you choose to turn it on, you’ll be able to capture any audio that’s playing on your iPhone, as well as your own voice. # How to Record Your iPhone Screen with a Computer

If you’re having trouble with the built-in Screen Recording feature, or if you want more advanced features like video editing and annotation, you may want to consider using a computer to record your iPhone screen. To do this, you’ll need to connect your iPhone to your computer using a USB cable, then open the QuickTime Player app. From there, you can select ‘File’ > ‘New Movie Recording’, then select your iPhone as the camera. You can then record your screen, and the video will be saved to your computer.
